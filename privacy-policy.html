Privacy Policy for Bibek's Chrome Extensions

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to all Chrome extensions developed and published by Bibek Chand Sah ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our extensions handle information.

Universal Privacy Commitment: All our Chrome extensions are designed with privacy-first principles. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All functionality is local to your device.

This policy covers all our Chrome extensions including:

  • Video Speed Controller - Enhanced video playback speed control with keyboard shortcuts
  • Custom New Tab - Personalized new tab page with custom shortcuts and themes
  • Future Productivity Extensions - Upcoming tools for web productivity and enhancement
  • Developer Tools - Extensions designed for web developers and designers
  • Utility Extensions - General-purpose browser enhancement tools

This list will be updated as we publish new extensions. All extensions follow the same privacy principles outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

Local Storage Only

Our extensions may store data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data varies by extension but may include:

  • User Preferences: Settings, configurations, and customizations you make
  • Application State: Current state of the extension to maintain functionality
  • User-Generated Content: Data you create or input within the extension
  • Website-Specific Settings: Per-domain or per-site preferences
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Custom key bindings and shortcuts
  • Theme/UI Preferences: Visual customizations and interface settings

What We DON'T Collect

  • We do NOT collect any personal information or personally identifiable information (PII)
  • We do NOT track your browsing activity or history
  • We do NOT send any data to external servers or third parties
  • We do NOT use analytics, tracking, or telemetry services
  • We do NOT access your personal files, emails, or sensitive data
  • We do NOT collect usage statistics or behavioral data
  • We do NOT use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • We do NOT access your camera, microphone, or location

Extension-Specific Data Collection

Some of our extensions may collect additional data to provide enhanced functionality. This data is always stored locally and never transmitted:

Custom New Tab Extension

This extension may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Personal Name: Your name (if provided) for personalized greetings
  • Profile Picture: Custom profile picture (if uploaded) stored locally
  • Website Shortcuts: Most visited websites configuration and custom bookmarks
  • Theme Preferences: Display preferences, themes, and visual customizations
  • Quick Access Links: Custom shortcuts and frequently used website links

Important: Even when collecting this personal data, it remains entirely on your device. We never access, transmit, or store this information on external servers.

Other Extensions

Future extensions may collect similar types of user-provided data for personalization, but will always follow these principles:

  • Data is only collected when explicitly provided by the user
  • All data remains stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Users can view, modify, or delete their data at any time

How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Maintain Functionality: Ensure the extension works as intended
  • Preserve Settings: Remember your preferences between browser sessions
  • Enhance User Experience: Provide personalized and consistent functionality
  • Store User Configurations: Keep your custom settings and configurations
  • Enable Features: Support advanced features that require state persistence
  • Improve Performance: Cache data locally to improve extension responsiveness

Important: All data processing happens locally on your device. No data is ever transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage APIs
  • No External Transmission: No data is ever transmitted to external servers, databases, or cloud services
  • No Third-Party Sharing: No data is shared with third parties, advertisers, or analytics services
  • Chrome Security: Data is protected by Chrome's built-in security mechanisms
  • User Control: You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time
  • No Backups: We do not create backups of your data on external servers

Data Deletion

You can delete all extension data by:

  1. Removing the extension from Chrome (recommended)
  2. Clearing browser extension data through Chrome settings
  3. Using the extension's built-in reset/clear data features (if available)

Custom New Tab Extension - Data Control

This extension provides additional data control options:

  • View Data: All stored data is visible through the extension interface
  • Modify Data: You can edit or update any stored information at any time
  • Selective Deletion: Delete specific data items (name, picture, shortcuts) individually
  • Complete Reset: Use the built-in "Clear Storage" feature to remove all data
  • Data Export: View all your data in a readable format (if feature is available)

Data Retention Policy

Your data is retained locally on your device until you:

  • Manually clear it using the extension's clear storage feature
  • Uninstall the extension
  • Clear your browser's local storage
  • Reset Chrome or clear all browser data

No Automatic Deletion: We do not automatically delete your data - you have complete control over retention.

Permissions Explained

Our extensions may request various permissions depending on their functionality. Here's what each permission means:

Common Permissions

Storage Permission

  • Purpose: Save your preferences and settings locally on your device
  • Data: Extension settings, user preferences, application state
  • Scope: Local device only, never transmitted externally

ActiveTab Permission

  • Purpose: Access the currently active tab when you interact with the extension
  • Data: Current page content for extension functionality
  • Scope: Only the active tab, only when you use the extension

Scripting Permission

  • Purpose: Inject scripts to provide extension functionality
  • Data: No data collection, only functionality enhancement
  • Scope: Limited to specific websites or user-initiated actions

Host Permissions (Website Access)

  • Purpose: Access specific websites to provide functionality
  • Data: Page content modification, no data collection
  • Scope: Limited to specified websites relevant to extension functionality

Extension-Specific Permissions

Some extensions may require additional permissions such as:

  • Tabs: To manage or interact with browser tabs
  • ContextMenus: To add right-click menu options
  • Notifications: To show system notifications
  • Bookmarks: To access or manage bookmarks (if applicable)
  • History: To access browsing history (if applicable)

Privacy Guarantee: Regardless of permissions requested, we never collect, store, or transmit personal data.

Third-Party Services

Our extensions do not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, external APIs, or tracking services. All functionality is self-contained within each extension. We do not use:

  • Google Analytics or similar analytics services
  • Advertising networks or tracking pixels
  • External databases or cloud storage
  • Social media integrations that collect data
  • Payment processors (our extensions are free)
  • Error reporting services that transmit data
